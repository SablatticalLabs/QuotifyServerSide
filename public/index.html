Quotify.it takes the random things you and your friends say, and randomly says them back to you.

With Quotify.it, you enter things your friends say on our mobile app, save it along with a snapshot, and we send it back to all of you via a group e-mail chain later on... when you least expect it.

It could be two days, a week, even a month! One day, you'll all wake up and find it right there in your inbox - a quote-of-the-day from your own life, shared with friends.
